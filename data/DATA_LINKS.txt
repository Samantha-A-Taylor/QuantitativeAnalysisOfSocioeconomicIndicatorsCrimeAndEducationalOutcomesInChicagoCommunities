DATA LINKS

Due to file size limitations, the datasets used in this project are not stored directly in this repository.
They are publicly available and can be accessed at the following sources:

1. Census Data – Selected Socioeconomic Indicators in Chicago (2008–2012)
   Source: City of Chicago Data Portal
   https://data.cityofchicago.org/Health-Human-Services/Census-Data-Selected-socioeconomic-indicators-in-C/kn9c-c2s2/about_data

2. Chicago Public Schools – Progress Report Cards (2011–2012)
   Source: City of Chicago Data Portal
   https://data.cityofchicago.org/Education/Chicago-Public-Schools-Progress-Report-Cards-2011-/9xs2-f89t/about_data

3. Crimes – 2001 to Present
   Source: City of Chicago Data Portal
   https://data.cityofchicago.org/Public-Safety/Crimes-2001-to-Present/ijzp-q8t2/about_data
